# AETERNA FAQ

## General

- **What is AETERNA?** A real-time crypto alert platform that aggregates 50+ sources and filters noise with AI.
- **Who is it for?** Active traders and analysts who need sub-second alerts and configurable filters.

## Account & Access

- **How do I sign up?** Use the Register flow from the landing page; email/password required.
- **How do I reset password?** Go to Settings → Account and use the password change form.
- **Why was I logged out?** Tokens expire or were cleared; sign back in. Admins can force logout for security.

## Alerts & Notifications

- **Why don’t I see alerts?** Check priority/source filters and that your connection is stable.
- **How do I reduce noise?** Use priority filters, dismiss irrelevant alerts, and keep a focused watchlist.
- **Do you support Telegram/email?** Yes—enable channels under Settings → Notifications.
- **What are quiet hours?** A schedule that pauses notifications between start/end times.

## Watchlist & Preferences

- **How do I add tokens?** Settings → Preferences → Watchlist; enter uppercase symbols (2–10 chars).
- **Why is my token rejected?** Symbols must be uppercase alphanumerics, 2–10 characters.

## Performance & Reliability

- **How fast are alerts?** Target sub-100ms from ingestion to display under normal load.
- **What if the service is down?** The UI shows errors/toasts; retry or contact support with a timestamp.

## Troubleshooting

- **I see “Offline” in the header. What now?** Check your internet, then click Refresh. If it persists, wait 30s for auto-reconnect or contact support with timestamp + browser.
- **Filters hide everything—how to reset?** Use the Clear Filters action (Dashboard empty state) or remove query params in Alert History.
- **Exports fail. Why?** Ensure filters return at least one row; if still failing, try again after 30s or reduce page size.

## Billing & Plans

- **Is there a trial?** Landing CTA offers a 14-day free trial (if enabled by backend plan).
- **Can I cancel anytime?** Yes; cancel via account management (backend/portal dependent).
- **Do you store payment details?** Payments are handled by the billing provider; AETERNA does not store card data directly.
- **How do I get invoices?** Use the billing portal link (if enabled) or request via support.

## Security & Privacy

- **What data do you store?** Account email, hashed credentials, alert preferences, and watchlist symbols. No private keys.
- **Do you support 2FA?** If enabled by backend, you’ll see the toggle in Settings → Account.
- **How do I report a security issue?** Email the security contact in the landing footer with reproduction steps.

## Support

- **How to contact support?** Use the primary support email listed on the landing page footer or in-app help.
- **Where to report bugs?** Provide steps, browser, and timestamp; include screenshots if possible.
