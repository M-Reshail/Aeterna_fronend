# Getting Started with AETERNA

## 1) Create your account

- Go to the landing page and click **Try Free for 14 Days**.
- Complete registration; verify email if required by backend.

## 2) Log in

- Use your credentials on the Login page.
- On first login, you start with default filters and no watchlist entries.

## 3) Set your notifications

- Navigate to **Settings → Notifications**.
- Enable Email/Telegram/Dashboard channels as needed.
- Configure quiet hours to pause alerts overnight.

## 4) Tune your watchlist

- Go to **Settings → Preferences → Watchlist**.
- Add uppercase symbols (2–10 chars). Keep it focused for signal quality.

## 5) Choose alert priorities

- In Dashboard/Alert History, filter by **HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW** to reduce noise.

## 6) Work the live feed

- Use the Dashboard to scan real-time alerts.
- Open details, dismiss noise, and mark items read to keep the queue clean.

## 7) Review history

- Open **Alert History** to search and filter past alerts for audits and backtesting.

## 8) Keep secure

- Change password in **Settings → Account**.
- Log out from the header menu when on shared devices.

## Quick tips

- Set quiet hours and channel preferences early to avoid alert fatigue.
- Keep your watchlist lean; fewer symbols = higher relevance.
- Dismiss or mark read to train your workflow and keep context tight.
